# Tạo query DQL từ danh sách các key
Do that in several steps. 
1) `^.*$` ⇒ `$& as "$&"`
2) `(?:\G|^\S*)\K\h+(?=.* as ".*"$)` ⇒ `-,`
3)  `(?:\G-|^)[^-]+(?=\S* as ".*"$)` ⇒ `\L$&`
